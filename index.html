Want to see your own messages here?

curl
from your terminal! Here's an example to create a new author: 

curl -i -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST -d '{"author_id": 2, "full_name": "董仲舒", "email": "dongzhonshu@qq.com"}' https://publicmessage.herokuapp.com/api/v0/author/

After you have an author id, you can go ahead and post your message: 

curl -i -H "Content-Type: application/json" -X POST -d '{"message_id": 3, "author_id": 4, "message": "good morning 5"}' https://publicmessage.herokuapp.com/api/v0/message/
